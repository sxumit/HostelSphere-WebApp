Features

HostelSphere is a modern hostel management platform built to simplify daily operations and administration. It brings room management, student records, fee tracking, and communication into one unified system. Designed for efficiency and ease of use, HostelSphere reduces manual effort and improves transparency. Our mission is to help hostels operate smarter while creating a better living experience for students.


Room Management

Easily manage rooms, occupancy, and availability in one place.

Student Records

Maintain complete student details securely with quick access.

Fee Tracking

Track hostel fees, dues, and payment history efficiently.

Complaints & Notices

Students can raise issues and receive important announcements.
